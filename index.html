Season of giving

Need to get gifts wrapped for the holidays? Want to donate to a worthy cause? Do both at this student-run charity event! USC Roski students will wrap your gifts in the choice of 13 original wrapping paper designs. All proceeds from this event will go towards California wildfire-relief efforts. Each sheet of wrapping paper is 29 inches by 20 inches.
Each sheet comes with complimentary gift wrapping!

What is “Wrap to Give?”

Wrap To Give is a team of design students that applies design-thinking to raise funds to help the victims of the northern California wildfires. All proceeds from Wrap To Give will go toward the California Community Foundation’s Wildfire Relief Fund .

This October alone, the wildfires in northern California killed 42 people and destroyed about 8,900 building structures, according to Cal Fire. To do our part in helping in the rebuilding and recovery efforts, our team has designed an assortment of 13 unique wrapping paper patterns. Celebrate the spirit of the holidays and make a difference in our community by contributing to a worthy cause. In exchange for your donation, you are aiding in:

  • the cleanup of debris and toxic fumes
  • supplementing medical costs for fire-related injuries
  • the rebuilding of public structures
  • providing trauma support for victims and their families
